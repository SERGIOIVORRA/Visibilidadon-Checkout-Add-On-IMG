Step‑by‑step guide
Add your U‑Extension to Checkout

Follow these steps to make your checkout block visible in Shopify's checkout editor. You’ll select the placement, pick App, and choose your app block.

This page defaults to English. Use the button at the top to read it in Spanish.
Quick path:
  1. In the checkout editor, go to the Main section where you want the block.
  2. Click Add block → App.
  3. Pick your app block (e.g., checkout‑ui) and click Save.

1) Open the checkout editor

From your Shopify admin, go to Settings → Checkout → Customize. The editor shows your checkout preview on the right and the structure on the left.

2) Choose where to place the block

In the left panel, select the area where your block should appear (e.g., Main under Payment).

You can add blocks to places like Main or the Order summary depending on your app’s target.

3) Click “Add block” → “App”

Press Add block and choose App. This opens the list of checkout UI extensions installed in your store.

4) Pick your U‑Extension block

Select your app block (e.g., checkout‑ui), configure any settings it exposes, and then click Save.

5) Test in a real checkout

Create a test draft order or use a development product, go through checkout, and confirm your block appears exactly where expected.
